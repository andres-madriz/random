### 2. NEIGHBORHOOD-BY-NEIGHBORHOOD CATALOG

⚓ Neighborhood A: Ribeira & Gaia Waterfront

The Vibe: A cinematic, high-contrast landscape of stacked multi-colored medieval facades mirroring across the golden hour currents of the Douro River.

Landmarks: Dom Luís I Iron Bridge Footway & Capela das Almas.

Dining Spots:
• *Casual:* DaTerra Ribeira Veg (Incredible all-you-can-eat plant-based buffet variations).
• *Sit-Down:* Com Cuore 100% GF (Safehaven pastry paradise; completely gluten-free traditional Pastel de Nata mapping).

Hidden Gem: *Armazém* — A cavernous, hidden architectural warehouse serving as an industrial vintage design collective and quiet terrace wine sanctuary.

🎨 Neighborhood B: Cedofeita & Clérigos District

The Vibe: The independent creative pulse of the city, lined with low-key art galleries, concept spaces, and leafy residential plazas.

Landmarks: Clérigos Baroque Bell Tower & Livraria Lello.

Dining Spots:
• *Casual:* Kind Kitchen Veg GF Options (High-energy vegan street food kitchen handling stellar clean tacos and bowls).
• *Sit-Down:* Época Café (Design-forward, organic plant-focused seasonal lunch menu emphasizing raw ingredients).

Hidden Gem: *Coração Alecrim* — An eco-conscious boutique tracking custom hand-crafted ceramics and local sustainable apparel independent lines.

### 5. LOCAL CULTURE & PACKING CHEAT SHEET

📜 Tipping & Etiquette Protocols

1. *The Couvert Rule:* The small olives, cheese, and bread brought to the table instantly are not free; if you consume them, they are charged per item. Politely pass them back if not desired.

2. *Tipping Strategy:* Tipping is not standard culture; round up the bill cleanly by 5-10% for exceptional sit-down group host service
