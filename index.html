### 5. LOCAL CULTURE & PACKING CHEAT SHEET

📜 Tipping & Etiquette Protocols

1. *The Couvert Rule:* The small olives, cheese, and bread brought to the table instantly are not free; if you consume them, they are charged per item. Politely pass them back if not desired.

2. *Tipping Strategy:* Tipping is not standard culture; round up the bill cleanly by 5-10% for exceptional sit-down group host service.

3. *Tone Volatility:* Keep voice tones measured in historical residential stone lanes; local neighborhoods appreciate low acoustic footprints at night.

🎒 Transit & Micro-Packing Essentials

Transit Strategy: Download the *Anda App* or buy the physical *Andante Card* right at the airport terminal entry gates for seamless tapping parameters.

1. Vibram-Soled Footwear: Porto’s historic wet cobblestones feature an aggressive incline slope; standard flat sneakers will lose friction instantly.

2. Polarized Sunglasses: The blinding white light reflecting off granite historical architecture and river waves requires technical optic filters.

3. Lightweight Windshell: Atlantic ocean winds cause sudden microclimate temperature drops along river corridors by sunset.
